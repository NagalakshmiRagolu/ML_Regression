About This Project

This project demonstrates two regression models:

  • SLR — Predict Salary from Years of Experience.
  • MLR — Predict Profit from R&D, Administration, Marketing, and State (0=New York, 1=California, 2=Florida).

How to use

  1. From Home click SLR or MLR.
  2. Enter numeric values in the form fields and click Predict.
  3. The prediction appears below the form (same page) in a box.
